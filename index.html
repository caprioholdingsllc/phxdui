We Handle All DUI Cases

First-Time DUI

If this is your first DUI arrest, you're facing serious consequences but also have the most options for defense and mitigation.

  • Challenge BAC test results
  • Question stop legality
  • Negotiate reduced charges
  • Minimize license suspension
  • Avoid jail time if possible

Second DUI

A second offense carries mandatory jail time and longer license revocation. Aggressive defense is critical.

  • Challenge prior conviction validity
  • Fight current charges vigorously
  • Minimize mandatory jail time
  • Work toward restricted license
  • Protect employment and livelihood

Felony DUI (Third or More)

Third offense or aggravated DUI charges are felonies with prison time. You need experienced legal representation immediately.

  • Fight felony charges
  • Seek charge reduction when possible
  • Minimize prison exposure
  • Explore alternative sentencing
  • Protect future record

Extreme/Super Extreme DUI

BAC over .15 or .20 carries enhanced penalties even on first offense. These charges require specialized defense.

  • Challenge breath/blood test accuracy
  • Question testing procedures
  • Fight mandatory jail time
  • Negotiate penalties
  • Preserve driving privileges
